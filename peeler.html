How to Start Your Peelers Social Club

1

Download the Peelers App

Get the app from Google Play or App Store and connect with Boby, your virtual AI security officer for your postcode.

2

Chat with Boby

Tell Boby you'd like to become a community organiser. She'll connect you with existing groups or help you start fresh.

3

Build Your Core Team

Recruit volunteers for key roles: Event Coordinator, Communications Manager, Business Liaison, and Activity Champions.

4

Start Simple

Begin with monthly morning teas or casual coffee meetups. Use old-school letter drops to personally invite neighbours.

5

Add Fun Activities

Launch community challenges like best front lawn competitions, Christmas light displays, and postcode photography contests.

6

Grow and Thrive

Expand programming based on resident interests while maintaining focus on genuine relationships and community care.
